THE STORY

Retaining the Octave's sleek, attractive lines, contemporary design and elegantly chic look, the Signature Series introduces newly engineered innovations in handcrafted Morel drivers for true audiophile-grade 20hz-20khz coverage.

The Octave Signature Series
on Youtube

STREAMLINED DESIGN
The Octave Signature Series is fully modular, with matching bookshelf, center and subwoofer speakers, to impeccably perform double service on both two-channel stereo and multi-channel home cinema. A dedicated subwoofer amplifier and minimalist solid metal stands round out the lineup.

LIGHTER, STRONGER, AND MORE COMFORTABLE
The Signature Series is defined by enhancements to the tweeter and woofer drivers and crossover networks, all painstakingly developed and refined in the Morel labs. These technical achievements have further distilled Morel's unique acoustic qualities across the entire sound spectrum, producing a realism that has to be heard to be believed. aluminum coils that are three times larger than conventional speakers, powerful and compact neodymium and hybrid magnet motors, handcoated Acuflex™ silk-dome tweeters and the most advanced, quality materials.

SPECS & FEATURES

Volume Limiting: 85 dB maximum volume - patent pending
Passive Noise Attenuation: Up to 33.4 dB
Frequency Response: 20-20,000 Hz
Drivers: 40 mm, moving coil dynamic type
Impedance: 32 Ω
Cord: 40" (1,016 mm) premium twin-lead cables
Plug: 90 degree stereo 1/8⊗ (3.5 mm) gold-plated with offset to allow use when protective phone cover is installed (patent pending)
